## Why pay for storage?

RAW is heavy — one frame is 20–60 MB. The editor and pipeline stay **free**; we charge
only to keep your originals safe in the cloud and swap them back on demand. You pay for
what actually costs us — not for the tools.

> Edit free. Pay to keep.
