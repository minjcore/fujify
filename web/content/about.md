# About

**Fujify** is a photo app for people who actually care about their images.

Most apps make you choose: powerful but heavy (and a monthly bill), or simple but
lossy. Fujify takes a different line — *edit free, share at real quality, keep your
originals.*

---

## What we believe

- **The tools should be free.** White balance, tone, presets, export — the craft of
  editing isn't where we charge you.
- **Quality is the point.** Social platforms recompress everything; we make sure the
  file you *have* is the best version that ever existed — 2K+, 4:4:4, EXIF intact.
- **Your originals stay yours.** RAW is heavy, so cloud storage is what we charge for —
  fair, because it's what actually costs us. See [Pricing](#/pricing).

> Edit. Share. Keep.

---

## How it's built

A single color engine (RAW decode, white balance, tone, presets, social export) drives
everything — the **Fuji-fy Studio** desktop app, the mobile app, and the command line.
The desktop editor runs natively on macOS (Dear ImGui, OpenGL & Vulkan) with the engine
bundled in — no setup.
